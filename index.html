About EYƎ

A contemporary art and design gallery like no other.

SeriEYE is a contemporary art and design gallery unlike any other. At the crossroads of a gallery, an experiential space, a lifestyle hub, and a celebration of gastronomy.

A nomadic contemporary art and design gallery with no fixed walls, it travels the world. Across the globe, it inhabits unique spaces that reflect its identity. SeriEYE brings together collectors and artists in immersive evenings, events, and dinners that highlight gastronomy — featuring the creative and generous cuisine of Chef Nicolas Zanel.

SeriEYE showcases only artworks, furniture, and objects selected with both intuition and discernment. Each piece is carefully chosen through my eye for its uniqueness, strength, and beauty — and also for its long-term potential, not in a speculative sense, but with enlightened boldness, as if I were acquiring it for my own home.

Yes, SeriEYE could be seen as an “idealized home,” because it brings together everything that matters deeply to me: art, design, meaningful encounters, and the joy of sharing a table.

"I was born Serieye, and my surname holds the very quality that defines me: the Eye. My life journey as a globetrotter in the service of beauty, fashion, lifestyle, and later art, has allowed me to sharpen this Eye — a compass in the fog — spotting emerging creators ahead of time, making choices with discernment, instinct, and conviction, both for myself and those around me, in a deeply human way. Intuitively, I have built a true global network of people who share my philosophy."

"It is this vision that I now dare to share with you — artists, clients, and friends — through this bold project, into which I pour all my discernment and insight, my heart and my energy: the SeriEYE project."

Florent Serieye, Founder

What We Do

A Free and Nomadic Gallery

Independent, flexible, and human-scale, SeriEYE travels and appears in cities that matter in the world of creation — following the rhythm of major global art events. It stops in places like Paris, Geneva, Athens, Mumbai, Rome, Dubai, and Nice, among others.

With every edition

a new space buzzing with contagious energy an unexpected setting transformed into a meeting point between contemporary design, emerging art, and gastronomy. Each edition takes place in a singular venue that reflects the cultural identity of the host city.

Collaborations in unique locations

Driven by a spirit of human and artistic convergence, SeriEYE partners with institutions and brands to host events in spaces like cultural venues within embassies or the headquarters of friendly brands. These collaborations give invited local designers and artists the opportunity to create interdisciplinary projects.

Meaningful encounters

Because each artwork tells the story of its creator, it sparks connection and weaves a deeply human network. Artists, collectors, and friends meet in a qualitative way, forging meaningful bonds that go far beyond a simple transaction between artist and buyer.

Food for the soul — and the senses

Because a complete experience must also be flavorful, gastronomy plays a central role — with the creative and generous cuisine of Chef Nicolas Zanel and/or local chefs who craft culinary experiences inspired by the artworks on display, offering guests the chance to quite literally taste the art.

Sustainability & Respect

Respect is our guiding principle. Each event integrates sustainable practices — using recycled materials for installations, and prioritizing local products for the culinary experiences.
