About EYƎ

A contemporary art and design gallery like no other.

SeriEYE is a contemporary art and design gallery like no other—a fusion of gallery space and experiential venue, blending art, lifestyle, and gastronomy.

A nomadic contemporary art and design gallery without fixed walls, SeriEYE travels the world. Across the globe, it inhabits unique spaces that reflect its identity; SeriEYE brings together collectors and artists in immersive evenings, events, and dinners that celebrate gastronomy featuring the creative and generous cuisine of Chef Nicolas Zanel.

SeriEYE presents only works, furniture, and objects chosen with intuition and reason. Carefully selected with my eye for their uniqueness, strength, and beauty, also evaluating their potential over time—not speculatively, but with enlightened boldness, as if I were acquiring them for my own personal living space.

Yes, SeriEYE could be an "idealized home," because SeriEYE catalyzes everything that matters to me: art, design, meaningful encounters, and the pleasure of the table.

"I was born Serieye, and my surname contains the quality that defines me: the Eye. My life journey as a globetrotter in the service of beauty, fashion, lifestyle, and then art, has allowed me to refine it—this eye as a compass in the fog, spotting emerging creators ahead of time, making choices of works with rigor, instinct, and conviction, for myself and for those close to me, in a deeply human way, intuitively creating a true global network of people who share my philosophy."

"It is this vision that I now dare to share with you—artists, clients, and friends—through this crazy project, into which I put all my rigor and insight, my heart and my energy: the SeriEYE project."

Florent Serieye, Founder

What We Do

A Free and Nomadic Gallery

Free, flexible, and human-sized, SeriEYE travels and appears in cities that matter for creativity, following the calendars of major global art events—for example, Paris, Geneva, Athens, Mumbai, Rome, Dubai, and Nice, among others.

Places Full of Energy

At each edition, a new place full of communicative energy—an unexpected space transformed into a meeting point for contemporary design, emerging art, and gastronomy. Each edition takes place in a unique setting, reflecting the cultural identity of the host city.

Unique Collaborations

In a spirit of human and artistic convergence, SeriEYE partners with other galleries, as well as organizations, companies, and brands, to organize events in places such as the cultural space of an embassy or a friendly brand's venue.

Precious Encounters

Because a work tells the story of its creator, it generates an experience and weaves a deeply human network between people: artists, clients, and friends meet in a qualitative way, forging a precious bond that goes beyond mere transactions.

Art Meets Gastronomy

A complete experience must also nourish the senses. Gastronomy holds a central place in the SeriEYE universe, with creative and generous menus imagined by Chef Nicolas Zanel—or by local chefs who design culinary experiences inspired by the exhibited works.

Sustainability & Respect

Respect—for people, places, and the planet—is a fundamental value of SeriEYE. Each edition incorporates sustainable practices: recycled materials for installations, local products favored in culinary experiences, and every decision guided by a sincere commitment to responsibility and authenticity.
